I am happy to be able to share my life with four wonderful birds! It is a real joy to spend time with them, to learn more about their care and their personalities, and to watch how they play and interact with each other.

Below is a little bit of info about each of my birds!


Zelda

Zelda is a female crimson bellied conure. She was born on the 21st August 2019, and has been with us since the 1st November 2019. She is incredibly friendly, intelligent, and energetic!



Ayato

Ayato is a male pied cockatiel. He has been with us since the 25th April 2019. He used to be incredibly fearful and nervous about leaving his cage, but he has since really come out of his shell! He loves Adora, and is much braver than he used to be (though he is still very cautious at times!)



Adora

Adora is a female pearl cockatiel. She has been with us since the 15th September 2019. She came to us in a very bad state, having been kept in terrible conditions and treated very poorly. But she has recovered fantastically! She is healthy, can fly very well, and is very curious and playful. She loves Ayato completely, and spends most of her time with him.



Allana

Allana is a female budgie. She has been with us since the 7th August 2019. She is brave and very capable, though she prefers to stay away from hands!
